WHY HARMONIZE
At Harmonize Design, we believe in thinking differently to reach and create not only a higher dwelling value but also unique living quality. We define UNIQUE that interior space must be a good living container, a reflection of individual. The relation among spaces inside a home must devote to the description by Louis Kahn architect, “A plan is a society of rooms.” While designing, we shall adopt none of embedded styles. We shall open the space and broaden the visual. Let the natural light in and create an even lighting. Underscore the concise line and shape to reduce the visual complexity. Apply varied finish materials and present the variation of textures to enhance the abundance in look-and-feel. Thus, each interior component speaks up itself and the whole interior presents a good combination of spaces in terms of calm, simple and low-loading, which fosters the interaction among rooms. Meanwhile, select furniture and lighting feature in the beginning, consider their locations and showcase them as pieces of art work so they become a part of interior. In the end, a proper but eye-catching decoration is needed to create a sense of geniality.
